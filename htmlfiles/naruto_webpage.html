About Naruto:

Naruto is a manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto that was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump. Met with immediate popularity following its initial publication in 1999, the series went on to receive an anime adaptation that commenced broadcasting in 2002. The manga concluded in 2014 with a total of 700 chapters. The total number of print copies sold globally exceeds 250 million, and even now Naruto events, games, merchandise, and more continue to be created and released for fans to enjoy a testament to the series’ enduring influence and popularity around the world.
It is the story of how Naruto an alone kid, fights through the toughest of situations in the Ninja world. And achieves his ultimate goal of being a ‘Hokage’ by never giving up on his dreams and believing in his comrades and himself.
Naruto anime consists of 720 episodes in total, the first part called Naruto has 220 episodes and the later part called Naruto Shippuden has 500 episodes.
